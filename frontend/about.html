About ContRec

Your personalized content recommendation system

ContRec is an innovative platform that helps you discover personalized content across multiple platforms based on your interests and preferences. Our system intelligently aggregates recommendations from various sources to provide you with content tailored specifically to your tastes.

Whether you're looking for the latest videos on YouTube, trending music on Spotify, popular posts on social media, or trending repositories on GitHub, ContRec brings it all together in one convenient feed.

Key Features

Multi-Platform Integration

Connect and aggregate content from multiple platforms including YouTube, Spotify, Netflix, Instagram, Facebook, and many more.

AI-Powered Recommendations

Our intelligent algorithms learn from your preferences to deliver highly personalized content recommendations.

Unified Search

Search across all your connected platforms simultaneously to find exactly what you're looking for.

Customizable Preferences

Fine-tune your experience by selecting your interests and adjusting recommendation preferences.

Real-Time Updates

Receive fresh recommendations that update in real-time based on your activity and preferences.

Privacy Focused

Your data remains secure with end-to-end encryption and strict privacy controls.
